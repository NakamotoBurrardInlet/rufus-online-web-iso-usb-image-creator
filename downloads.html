How to Create Your Bootable USB

Because of web security, we can't directly write to your USB drive. Please follow these instructions to create your bootable drive using a free and trusted tool.

Using BalenaEtcher (Recommended for all OS)

  1. Download and install BalenaEtcher.
  2. Open BalenaEtcher.
  3. Click "Flash from file" and select the ISO you just downloaded.
  4. Click "Select target" and choose your USB drive.
  5. Click "Flash!" to begin the process.
